The first ever Houston Teen Hackathon, hosted at the University of Houston Victoria - Katy, for all high schoolers in Greater Houston interested in Computer Science and STEM, with guest speakers, prizes, and more. A fun, educational, and inspirational event for all coders - experienced and novice alike. Presented in collaboration with Valence8.

The USNAO was formed in 2019 in collaboration with Rice University and Texas Southern University. Every year the USNAO will send five top competitors to the International Astronomy Olympiad, organized by the Institute of Solid State Physics. The 24th IAO will be held in Piatra Neamt, Romania, from October 19-27.

Voyage Mentorship is an organization whose goal is to foster a competitive edge in young students interested in competing at the highest level by partnering qualified and award-winning tutors with the passionate, high-achieving students that best suit them.
